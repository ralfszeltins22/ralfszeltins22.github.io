Minecraft Online Launcher is a launcher made by ralfszeltins2. You can use this to play Minecraft in school or on devices that you cannot install Minecraft on.


Why isn't 'Launch in New Window' working?

Your browser is most likely blocking popups from this website.

To fix this, find the popups blocked icon. It is most likely on the right of the web address field.

Click on the icon, then click Allow Popups, then Done. That should fix the problem.
